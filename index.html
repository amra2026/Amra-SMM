About

From SEO to Social, With the Same Eye for Detail

My career started in 2012 as a Virtual Assistant. Since then I've worked as a customer service representative, recruiter, SEO specialist, and digital marketing professional — picking up most of it by doing the work, not by sitting through a course on it.

Social media management became a steady part of that work along the way. Today I manage it end-to-end: planning content calendars, writing captions, designing graphics in Canva, editing short clips in CapCut, scheduling posts, replying to comments and messages, and reporting on what's working.

I currently manage social strategy and content for a wellness and yoga brand across Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest, and support Pinterest content for a national HVAC and air filtration brand. My SEO background carries over too — I think about searchable captions and Pinterest SEO, not just what looks good in the grid.

Process

How a Week of Content Actually Gets Made

Plan

I build the content calendar around brand pillars, what's coming up, and what's performed well before.

Create

Captions get written, graphics get designed in Canva, and short clips get edited in CapCut.

Schedule

Posts get queued for when each platform's audience is actually online.

Engage

Comments and messages get replies, and I look for conversations worth joining.

Report

I check each platform's own analytics for what worked, then adjust the next calendar.

My approach to AI tools

I use ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini to speed up brainstorming and first drafts. The final caption, edit, and posting decision are always mine.
